Taco Seasoning

Description

This is my preferred recipe to use for making a homemade taco seasoning mix.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp sea salt
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a bowl and mix
  2. Store in an airtight container
  3. For every pound of ground meat, use two tablespoons of the mixture
