Shakshuka

Description

This is a recipe for making shakshuka, a delicious breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder
  • 28 oz can whole peeled tomatoes (preferably San Marzano)
  • 6 large eggs
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)
  • 1 bunch fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in large saute pan on medium heat. Add bell pepper and onion and cook for 5 minutes, or until onion becomes translucent
  2. Add garlic and spices and cook an additional minute
  3. Pour can of tomatoes and juice into pan and break down tomatoes using large spoon. Season with salt and pepper and bring sauce to a simmer
  4. Use the large spoon to make wells in the sauce and crack eggs into the wells. Cook eggs for 5-8 minutes, or until done to your liking
  5. Garnish with chopped cilantro and parsley before serving
