Deviled Eggs

Description

This is a recipe for making deviled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • paprika, for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil. Reduce heat to low (or off) and place eggs in water for 14 minutes
  2. While the eggs are cooking, prepare an ice bath and set to the side. After 14 minutes, transfer the eggs to the ice bath
  3. Once the eggs are cooled, peel them and slice in half lengthwise. Remove the yolk and place them in a bowl.
  4. Mash the yolks and add the mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Stir until smooth.
  5. Use a spoon to add a portion of the yolk mixture into each egg white half. Sprinkle on paprika to garnish, and serve.
